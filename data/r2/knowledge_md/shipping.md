**AI NOTES:** This is the official BeauTeas shipping policy. Use it to answer all delivery questions. Do not guess delivery times or costs, and do not offer expedited or international shipping unless it is listed here.

## Where We Ship

BeauTeas currently ships within the United States (all 50 states). We do not offer international shipping at this time.

## Processing & Handling

Orders are packed and shipped Monday through Friday. Orders placed before 12 PM Pacific typically ship within 1–2 business days. You'll receive a confirmation email with tracking as soon as your order is on its way.

## Shipping Costs

- Orders under $50: $5.95 flat-rate shipping
- Orders $50 and over: FREE standard shipping
- Subscription orders always ship free

## Estimated Delivery

Standard shipping is delivered by USPS, typically within 3–7 business days after your order ships, depending on your location. Delivery times are estimates and can vary due to carrier or weather delays.

## Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, we'll email you a tracking link. If you have a BeauTeas account, you can also find tracking in your order history.
